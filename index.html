About Us


We founded Impulse Prep with a simple purpose: to provide high-quality college preparatory coaching for our clients. Our elite team of six spans a broad array of subjects, from advanced mathematics to the humanities. We are passionate not only about our own studies but about forwarding our success to the next generation of learners.
